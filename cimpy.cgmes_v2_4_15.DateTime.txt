class cimpy.cgmes_v2_4_15.DateTime.DateTime

   Date and time as "yyyy-mm-ddThh:mm:ss.sss", which conforms with ISO
   8601. UTC time zone is specified as "yyyy-mm-ddThh:mm:ss.sssZ". A
   local timezone relative UTC is specified as "yyyy-mm-ddThh:mm:ss
   .sss-hh:mm". The second component (shown here as "ss.sss") could
   have any number of digits in its fractional part to allow any kind
   of precision beyond seconds.
